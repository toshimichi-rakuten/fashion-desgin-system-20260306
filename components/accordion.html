How does Buyer Protection work?
Rakuma Buyer Protection covers you if an item doesn't arrive or is significantly different from the listing description. You have 72 hours to raise a dispute.
What fees does Rakuma charge?
Rakuma charges a 3.5% service fee on completed transactions. Listing items is completely free.
Can I return an item?
Returns are at the seller's discretion. Buyer Protection applies if an item is not as described.
