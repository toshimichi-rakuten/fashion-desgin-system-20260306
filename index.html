Accordion / FAQ

Expandable content panels for FAQs, product details, and settings. Smooth open/close animation with keyboard support.

How does Buyer Protection work?
Rakuma Buyer Protection covers you if an item doesn't arrive or is significantly different from the listing description. You have 72 hours after delivery to raise a dispute. We hold your payment securely until you confirm receipt.
What fees does Rakuma charge?
Rakuma charges a 3.5% service fee on completed transactions. Listing items is completely free. Shipping costs depend on the carrier and package size — sellers typically cover this in the item price.
How do I verify item authenticity?
Look for the "AUTHENTIC" badge on listings. These sellers have passed our verification process. For luxury items over ¥30,000, we offer an optional physical authentication service for an additional fee.
Can I return an item?
Returns are at the seller's discretion. If an item is not as described, Buyer Protection applies and we'll mediate the dispute. Condition disagreements, sizing issues, and change of mind are not covered by Buyer Protection.
