Buying on Rakuma

How does Buyer Protection work?
Rakuma Buyer Protection covers every purchase made through our platform. If your item doesn't arrive, or is significantly different from the listing description, you're fully covered. Raise a dispute within 72 hours of delivery (or expected delivery) and our team will investigate. If the claim is valid, you'll receive a full refund including shipping. Your payment is held securely in escrow until you confirm safe receipt.
How do I track my order?
Once the seller ships your item, you'll receive a tracking number via email and in-app notification. Go to My Orders and tap "Track Package" to see real-time shipping status. Most domestic Japan shipments arrive within 2–5 business days.
Can I return an item I bought?
Returns are at the seller's discretion for change-of-mind purchases. However, if the item is not as described (wrong size, undisclosed damage, not authentic), you are covered by Buyer Protection for a full refund. Contact the seller first — most issues can be resolved directly.
How do I make an offer?
On any product listing, click "Make an Offer" and enter your proposed price. The seller will receive a notification and can accept, decline, or counter-offer within 24 hours. Once both parties agree, payment is processed automatically.

Selling on Rakuma

How much does it cost to sell?
Listing items on Rakuma is completely free. When an item sells, Rakuma charges a 3.5% service fee. This covers Buyer Protection, secure payments, and our 24/7 support team. There are no hidden fees or monthly subscription costs.
When do I receive payment?
Payment is released to your Rakuma balance 3 business days after the buyer confirms receipt (or after the dispute window closes). From your balance, you can withdraw to your Japanese bank account within 1–3 business days. A minimum withdrawal of ¥1,000 applies.
What items can I sell on Rakuma?
Rakuma specialises in fashion — clothing, shoes, bags, accessories, and beauty items. Items must be in sellable condition (not damaged beyond what's disclosed in the listing). Counterfeit goods, hazardous materials, and illegal items are strictly prohibited and will result in account suspension.
How do I take great listing photos?
Listings with 5+ photos sell 3x faster. Use natural light, a clean background, and photograph the item from multiple angles including close-ups of any brand labels, condition issues, and unique details. For bags and shoes, always include a photo next to a common reference object for scale.
