Common Questions

Can I cancel anytime?
Yes! Cancel anytime from your account settings. Your plan continues until the end of your billing period. No cancellation fees.
What happens to my listings if I downgrade?
If you have more than 20 listings when downgrading to Free, your newest listings will be temporarily hidden until you're within the free tier limit. You choose which to keep active.
Is the 14-day trial really free?
Absolutely. No credit card required to start your trial. After 14 days, you can choose to subscribe or return to the Free plan — no charges either way.
