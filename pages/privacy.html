1. What We Collect

We collect information you provide directly (name, email, payment info), information generated by your use of Rakuma (listings, transactions, messages), and technical data (IP address, device type, browser).

2. How We Use Your Data

We use your data to operate the marketplace, process payments, prevent fraud, send transaction emails, and improve our services. With your consent, we may send marketing communications.

  • Process and fulfil orders
  • Verify identity and prevent fraud
  • Personalise your feed and recommendations
  • Send important service notifications

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We share data with payment processors, shipping partners, and cloud infrastructure providers as necessary to operate the service. All third parties are bound by data processing agreements.

4. Your Rights

Under Japanese law (APPI) and applicable regulations, you have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@rakuma.jp.

5. Security

We use industry-standard security measures including 256-bit SSL encryption, secure data centres, and regular security audits to protect your information. No system is 100% secure, but we take all reasonable precautions.

6. Cookies

We use essential cookies (required for the site to function), analytics cookies (to understand usage), and preference cookies (to remember your settings). You can manage cookie preferences in your browser settings.

7. Contact

For privacy questions, contact our Data Protection Officer at privacy@rakuma.jp.
